The benchmark of
health-preserving care

Adhering to the standard of safety and infection control
gives you theconfidence that your facility has achieved a
benchmark of childcare health and safety.

Strengthening protections of children and staff.

Given the preponderance of risk factors in childcare settings, we have developed the health-preserving care Standard. Child care facility managers voluntarily take on enormous responsibility in exchange for the fulfilment that comes with the important role of overseeing the care of children. The Health Preserving Childcare standard builds on that dedication by empowering you to take the essential steps towards effectively mitigating risk of infection in your facility. As such getting accredited in health Preserving child care standard is the single most important thing you can do to advance health and safety of children and staff under your care and supervision. HSCA accreditation, shows your commitment to safeguarding the health and wellbeing of the children under your care, as well as the entire childcare community. Such commitment resonates not only with the parents of children under your supervision, but with your staff, government, and the general public.

Get an edge on the ongoing
infection risk in your childcare facility

The Health Preserving Care Standard is the first of its kind to take a holistic and comprehensive approach to tackling infection within childcare settings. The Standard will enable you to:
• Survey the ongoing risk of infection within your facility and
•Cultivate a deeper understanding of infectious disease risks in childcare
•Utilize effective utilizes effective and trackable measures to control and prevent disease transmission, which unnecessarily put the health of children and employees at risk.

Potential growth of your facility

Conforming to the Standard means in your facility the healthier and safer care of children is at the forefront of every decision made. This provide assurance to parents that you’re doing what’s best by their children and keeping them away from potential harm in their absence. This is likely to motivate discerning parents to seek out and select your childcare for their children, which would result in the growth or your facility.

Raising the global benchmark

Each time a childcare facility becomes accredited, it raises the global benchmark of health-preserving care.
