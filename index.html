First step: Awareness Phase

Discover the infections risks in your
childcare facility

The awareness phase is the first step in preparing your facility for optimum infection control. This phase is specially designed to enable you as a childcare manager to identify and uncover critical knowledge gaps among your staff regarding risk exposure. Online pre-assessment survey also helps staff to recognize any misconceptions surrounding the prevention of infection, and presents them with new learning opportunities.

Second Step: Certification Phase

Become qualified in optimum infection
control and prevention practices

Your facility’s doorway to healthier and safer care lack of knowledge

Lack of knowledge in infection preventive practice limits your employees` ability to control the spread of infections. Knowledge of infection control is a great step in rooting out the causes of the spread of diseases and their risk to children and employees. Certification phase is there for specially designed soundly qualify your daycare staff in the five core areas of infection control in childcare settings.

Training reduces guesswork, replacing it with confidence and proactive conduct on the part of your employees. This lowers employee stress and motivates your employees to thrive. Appropriate training for a given scenario can even save lives.

Infection preventive training also gives your daycare a clear performance path for your employees, enabling them to make infection preventive choices which are intrinsically linked with a healthier childcare environment.

Designed to complement daycare staff’s busy schedules

Because we’re fully aware that your workers are usually busy taking care of children, we specifically made the certification component of the accreditation program online-based. Daycare staff will be able to do their learning at home, on the move, or wherever suits them best.

Facilitate best practices for the entire childcare community and impact the next generation

To minimizes potential for further spread of that disease to family of children who attend childcare and also to enable better decision making when confronted with infectious disease at home, parents also have open access to instruction courses on optimum infection control and prevention. Children who observe proper infection-prevention practices in use both at home and elsewhere will be learning life skills at a young age that they’ll be able to take with them for the rest of their lives.

Infection preventive Task Manager: Implement optimum infection control strategies

Encouraging and verifying infection protocol is the cornerstone of a healthy childcare setting. The Task Management component allows you to implement a strategy that affords you the peace of mind that tasks geared towards risk mitigation and infection control are executed to your specification every time, and notifies you when demands are not being met.

Infection preventive Communication manager
